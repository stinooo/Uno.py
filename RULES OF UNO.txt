Goal:
You start with 7 cards your goal is to play your hand empty faster than your opponent.

Rules/Mechanics:
FILL the names to start playing.
CLICK next turn if u want to give turn to the next player.
CLICk take card if u want a card
player 2 above.
player 1 at the bottem.
You can throw a card if it is the color, number, of symbol otherwise if you dont have any cards to play you wil need to "Take a card".
If you play "Skip" card of "Reverse" card then you play directly second card if not you will draw a card after clicking next turn.
If you play "+2 Draw" card then in opponent's turn your opponent will draw 2 cards and he will be not able to throw a card so he will need to click on next turn.
If you play "+4 Wild Card" you can pick a color but cant play directly another card with this color and your opponent on his turn will draw 4 cards and he will be not able to throw a card so he need to use next turn.
